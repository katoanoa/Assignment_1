Project Idea

  • Overview

    • My project will be a companion app for individuals who are Muslim, new to Islam, or are interested in the Islamic faith. The religion is very vast and often time plagued by misunderstandings due to lack of knowledge or pure misinformation. New Muslims can find themselves overwhelmed by how much information there is out there and often times need someone who is practicing guiding them and get them more familiar with all the practices, however not everyone can have such an individual help them and it’s hard to have someone to be there at all times. The app will be there to outline the basics that a Muslim would have to do on the day to day such as the daily prayers (which are mandatory), and other voluntary things that are a bit confusing to follow due to specific timings and other aspects that make it difficult for even me, a person who’s been practicing their whole life, to follow. I hope that this makes it easier for the everyday Muslim to track their daily tasks and make it easy for the new convert to get accustomed to the faith.


  • Motivation

    • Islam is a religion that is slowly increasing in size as the years go along. With a steady increase in Australia by almost 27% according to the Australian Bureau of Statistics in the 2016 Census. Even increasing by 34.6% in 2021. So not even considering the increasing population in Australia this doesn’t even include the number overseas. With most Islamcentric apps focusing on recitation of the Quran(the Islamic holy book) and muslims who already have an understanding of what they need to do, not many focus on people who are completely in the dark. I think there has to be a better beginner friendly focused application that can also be created to cater to the individual person as the Muslim population is vast and cultural practices differ around the world, there needs to be a baseline that is based on only faith that can be used by users globally.


  • Description

    • Essentially I hope to focus on a few things that can be separated into 3 parts. A tracker feature to help individuals keep track of mandatory tasks that will be based on local time and date. With a primary focus on daily prayers as a guaranteed baseline. With an alarm to let an individual know when exactly it time for prayer. In addition, it will have an option to include voluntary practices that can differ on time of day and time of year. These things have often specific timings which can become overwhelming when you have done nothing of it and are attempting it all for the first time. As a new convert you might start off having only the daily prayers listed as a reminder and with time as you come to familiarize yourself with the schedule you can start to add more things. Essentially having an experience that caters to your own religious journey. And in this regard even Muslims who have been practicing for a long time can just add increasingly to match what their current schedule.

    In addition to this a map feature that will outline local mosques/prayer areas and local Islamic community centers that individuals can go to pray or look to participate in community events and activities. In addition to this the profile of local imams that they can seek to contact on queries or general help that they might need. It also is hard to find Halal food (food that is permissible a Muslim to eat) so listing on map for more details such as local halal certified restaurants, markets, and butchers. As this is something someone new to Islam will have to quickly get used to and it might be a bit confusing trying to figure out where you can comfortably buy food from because as someone who has been Muslim all my life that is certainly something difficult for even us to do sometimes.

    And finally, a dua (supplication) feature detailing different prayers that you can recite daily that will be filtered according to the need and context of the situation. There are many supplications that an individual can recite during the day (which are not mandatory) but they are so extensive that there is one for almost any specific thing that you might encounter. A potential search bar with keywords that might be able to specifically select a supplication meant for your current scenario with English and Arabic translations. Alongside with frequently asked questions about the Islamic faith that would be useful to people interested in Islam and new converts. And depending on how much we have to work with it can become as detailed as possible. Adding more and more frequently asked questions that even the everyday Muslim might be confused by.
